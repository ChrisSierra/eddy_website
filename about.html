About Me

The Law Offices of Eddy O. Marban has focused its practice on Florida and Federal employment law. Our firm represents clients in various employment cases, including overtime wages, minimum wages, unpaid wages, discrimination, sexual harassment, retaliation, whistleblower retaliation, executive compensation, unpaid commissions and other employment related matters. We strive to maintain high quality representation and attention regardless of the size of your claim. We utilize a team and personal contact approach towards litigating client’s claims. Our attorneys are fluent in both English and Spanish. To learn more about your rights click on the practice areas, or contact us today.
