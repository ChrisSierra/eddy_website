Eddy O. Marban, Esq. has 29 years of experience as an attorney at law. He is certified by the National Institute for Trial Advocacy in intensive professional trial skills. He has represented thousands of clients in employment matters. He is a public speaker on continuing legal education courses.
He graduated from the University of Miami, B.S., cum laude, in 1980. He then attended law school in Boston, Massachusetts, at the University of Suffolk and received his juris doctorate degree in 1984.
